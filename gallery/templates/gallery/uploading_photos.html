Contribute Photos

Want to add photos to the family archive? Use the upload link below. We review everything before it appears on the site.

Open upload folder →
Tip: You can upload from your phone directly using the Drive app.

What to upload

  • Family photos, events, holidays, school pics, vacations, etc.
  • Scans are great — 300dpi is perfect for most prints.
  • JPEG is fine; TIFF is welcome for high-quality scans.

Helpful info to include

If you can, put this in the filename or add a short note in a text file. If not you can comment on the photo once it is posted for admins to update tags.

What happens next

  1. We review uploads for duplicates and quality.
  2. We organize, create web + thumbnail versions, and tag them.
  3. Then they appear in the archive for browsing.
Nothing is published automatically — uploads are reviewed first.
